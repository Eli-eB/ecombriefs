Frequently Asked Questions

Before you ask

Do I need to do anything?

Just give us admin access to your store (Shopify, WooCommerce, etc.). We do all the work. We'll notify you by email when it's ready.

What if I don't have an audit?

You can request your free audit at ecombriefs.io and come back here with the report. We need the audit to know what to fix.

When do I pay?

After reviewing your request, we'll send you the correction plan + secure payment link (Stripe). You don't pay until you see exactly what we're going to do.

What if my store has many issues?

The Quick Wins plan has a limited scope (10 pages). The Full plan covers the entire store. If your case is very complex, we'll tell you before charging.

Will there be meetings or calls?

No. Everything is managed via email. We adapt to your schedule, not the other way around.

What about the guarantee?

If your EcomBriefs score doesn't improve after the implementation, we'll refund 100%. We measure with the same tool we use for the audit.
